# KCA UNIVERSITY (KCAU) STUDENT EMAIL SETUP GUIDE

## 1. Student Email Address Format
- **Platform:** Google / Gmail (www.gmail.com).
- **Username Format:** Your full registration number followed by `@students.kcau.ac.ke`.
- **Formatting Rule:** Remove any slashes (`/`) from your registration number when typing the email.
- **Example:** If your Student Registration number is **23/00095**, your email address will be **2300095@students.kcau.ac.ke**.

## 2. Default Password and First-Time Login
- **Initial Password:** Your default password is your **Date of Birth**.
- **Password Format:** Must be entered as **DD/MM/YYYY** (including the forward slashes).
- **Format Examples:** 
    - If born on Dec 1st, 2000, use: `01/12/2000`
    - If born on July 26th, 2003, use: `26/07/2003`
- **Login Steps:** 
    1. Go to `gmail.com`.
    2. Enter your student email address.
    3. Enter the default Date of Birth password.
    4. Read and click **ACCEPT** on the Organization's Policy page.

## 3. Mandatory Security Updates
- **Password Change:** Upon your first successful login, the system will require you to create a new, strong password. Click **Change Password** to save your new credentials.
- **Account Recovery:** You are encouraged to add a **recovery email** address. Click **Update** to add one or **Confirm** to proceed to your inbox. This helps you regain access if you forget your password in the future.

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- **Q: Where do I log in to my KCAU student email?**
  A: You log in via Gmail at gmail.com.
- **Q: My registration number has a slash (e.g., 22/01234), how do I type it?**
  A: Drop the slash. It becomes 2201234@students.kcau.ac.ke.
- **Q: What is the format for the default KCAU email password?**
  A: The format is Day/Month/Year (DD/MM/YYYY).
