# KCA UNIVERSITY (KCAU) SCHOOL OF TECHNOLOGY FEE STRUCTURE 2024-2025

## 1. General Fee Information
- **Tuition Fee Rate:** Kshs. 11,500 per unit (unless otherwise stated).
- **Attachment Fee:** Kshs. 11,500 (standard for most BSc programs).
- **Installment Plan:** Fees are payable in 3 installments: 
    1. 1st Installment: 50%
    2. 2nd Installment: 30%
    3. 3rd Installment: 20%
- **Note:** Installment payments carry an extra charge of Kshs. 1,500.

---

## 2. Degree Programmes - Group A
**Degrees:** BSc in Applied Computing, Bachelor of Business Information Technology (BBIT), BSc in Data Science.
- **Basis:** Calculation assumes 7 units per trimester.
- **Trimester 1 Total Fees:** Kshs. 92,310 (Admin: 11,810 + Tuition: 80,500).
- **Trimesters 2 to 8 Total Fees:** Kshs. 90,010 per trimester (Admin: 9,510 + Tuition: 80,500).
- **Industrial Attachment Fee:** Kshs. 11,500.

## 3. Degree Programmes - Group B
**Degrees:** BSc in Information Security & Forensics (BSc. ISF), BSc in Software Development.
- **Basis:** Calculation assumes 8 units per trimester.
- **Trimester 1 Total Fees:** Kshs. 104,240 (Admin: 12,240 + Tuition: 92,000).
- **Trimesters 2 to 8 Total Fees:** Kshs. 101,940 per trimester (Admin: 9,940 + Tuition: 92,000).
- **Industrial Attachment Fee:** Kshs. 11,500.

## 4. Degree Programmes - Group C
**Degrees:** BSc in Information Technology, BSc in Information and Communication Technology (ICT).
- **Basis:** Calculation assumes 6 units per trimester.
- **Trimester 1 Total Fees:** Kshs. 80,380 (Admin: 11,380 + Tuition: 69,000).
- **Trimesters 2 to 8 Total Fees:** Kshs. 78,080 per trimester (Admin: 9,080 + Tuition: 69,000).
- **Industrial Attachment Fee:** Kshs. 11,500.

## 5. Degree Programmes - Group D
**Degree:** BSc in Gaming and Animation Technology.
- **Trimester 1 Total Fees:** Kshs. 104,240 (8 units).
- **Trimesters 2 to 6 Total Fees:** Kshs. 101,940 per trimester (8 units).
- **Trimesters 7 and 8 Total Fees:** Kshs. 90,010 per trimester (7 units).
- **Industrial Attachment Fee:** Kshs. 11,500.

---

## 6. Certificate and Diploma Programmes
**Degrees:** Certificate/Diploma in Business Information Technology (CBIT/DBIT), Certificate/Diploma in Information Technology (CIT/DIT).

- **Module 1 (DBIT 1 / CBIT 1):** Total Kshs. 39,900 (Admin: 9,900 + Tuition: 30,000).
- **Modules 2 to 4 (DBIT/DIT 2-4):** Total Kshs. 37,900 per module (Admin: 7,900 + Tuition: 30,000).
- **Module 5 (DBIT 5 / DIT 5):** Total Kshs. 43,900 (Includes Kshs. 6,000 Attachment fee).
