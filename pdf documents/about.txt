# KCA UNIVERSITY (KCAU) GENERAL INFORMATION & PROSPECTUS

## 1. University Identity and History
- **Full Name:** KCA University (KCAU)
- **Status:** Private, non-profit institution of higher learning.
- **Founded:** 1989 as Kenya College of Accountancy (KCA) by ICPAK.
- **Charter Status:** Granted Letter of Interim Authority (LIA) in 1996; awarded full University Charter by the Kenyan Government on March 1, 2013.
- **Accreditations:** Accredited by the Commission for University Education (CUE); Member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU).
- **Awards:** Best Private University in Kenya (Federation of Kenya Employers, 2018); Top Private University in Employability (British Council Report, 2016).
- **Leadership:** Vice-Chancellor: Prof. Isaiah Wakindiki.
- **Location:** Nairobi, Kenya (Coordinates: 1°15′11″S 36°51′36″E).

## 2. Vision, Mission, and Core Values
- **Vision:** To be an innovation-driven, globally competitive business and technology university promoting integrity, diversity, and teamwork.
- **Mission:** To be a creative business and technology university, training employable and market-driven graduates for sustainable economic development.
- **Core Values:** Professionalism, Integrity, Innovation, Community, and Equity.

## 3. Campuses and Facilities
### 3.1 Campus Locations
- **Main Campus:** Along Thika Road, Survey.
- **Town Campus:** Nairobi CBD.
- **Kitengela Campus:** Kitengela.
- **Western Campus:** Kisumu.

### 3.2 Key Facilities
- **Library (Martin Oduor-Otieno Library):** Ultra-modern facility seating 1,500 students; features e-library center and high-speed wireless internet.
- **Tuition Complex:** Accommodates 3,000+ students; contains 50 classrooms, 9 state-of-the-art computer labs, and 5 lecture theatres with modern ICT integration.
- **Holistic Support:** Professionally run counselling unit, university clinic, and a well-equipped gym.

## 6. Application Process and Banking Details

### 6.1 How to Apply
1. **Form:** Download from website or pick up from any campus.
2. **Submission:** Submit to an enrollment coordinator at any campus or via post.
3. **Application Fee:** KSh 1,000.
4. **Documents Required:** High school certificates (KCSE/IGCSE), Academic transcripts, National ID/Passport/Birth Certificate, and 2 passport photos.
